Questions you may have


Do I need to be there when we arrive

No you don't have to be at home when we arrive- as long as access is available to rear of property all work can begin

Do I need to move anything out of the way

Yes please, remove all the children's toys, delicate plants and any garden ornaments which may present a trip hazard

How often should I have my gutters cleaned

We would recommend to have your gutters cleaned every 6 to 12 months, it helps them to perform more effectively in heavy rains. Also keeping the gutters clean prevent gutter seal cracks and cutting down on leaks and helps eliminate damage through leaks
