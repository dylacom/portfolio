Conservatory Cleaning

We clean, polish and apply stay clean coating on conservatory roof, finials, gutters windows and frames

Conservatory roof- cleaned using an active cleaner & thermo pure reach & wash system all windows & frames are polished & polymer coated & all guttering vacuum cleaned to remove any debris.


Note:- No pressure washers are used for cleaning as this not only damages the surface of the U.P.V.C but can also damage the seals of the double glazed units & can also blast water at high pressure under the eaves of the conservatory causing flooding & electrical damage
